About Us

Gifty Gift Shop was started in 2001 with the aim to making the process of gift giving much easier. Prior to this website, customers had to physically visit the shop to view our products and make purchases of the products they find to their liking. All in all, pre website and with a website, we aim to make your shopping experience very easy and smooth. We assure you that our products are of a high quality and are certified by the local bureau of standards. We also deliver the products to your doorstep in a good condition.
